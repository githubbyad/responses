Email not received


Hi,

Third party emails such as AOL, Yahoo, Gmail etc. constantly change their email policies and as a result, such emails may not be delivered. 

For example, if you have xyz@aol.com under "Forms" screen, bulletlink server will compose email "FROM: xyz@aol.com" and going to "TO: your client email". This email may not be delivered as "FROM: xyz@aol.com" is coming from bulletlink servers as compared to AOL servers. 

We recommend you use your domain related emails such as editor@YourSite.com etc. to avoid submission issues.  Please go ahead and change the AOL, Yahoo or Gmail email to your own email that is associated with your site such as editor@YourSite.com under "Forms" screen.

Thank you.

Make Restrictive Site


Hi,

A) First, you need to enable restriction access on your site by following below steps,

    1. Click on "Settings" icon from "Site Manager".

    2. Choose "Site Access = Restricted Access".

    3. Click on "submit".

B) After that, you need to restrict specific sub-menu pages by following below steps,

    1. Click on "Menus" icon from "Site Manager".

    2. Click on any specific menu.

    3. Click "edit" on any sub-menu.

    4. Choose "Restrict Access = Yes".

    5. Click on "submit".

Repeat the same steps for other sub-menu pages.

C) Now, you need to restrict articles by following below steps,

    1. Click on "Articles" icon from "Site Manager".

    2. Click "edit" on any article.

    3. Put {{more}} command anywhere under "Body" field. Text above this command is visible to all your visitors.

    4. Click on "submit".

Repeat the same steps to restrict other articles.

Please visit your homepage and press Ctrl+F5 or click on reload icon on your browser to see the updated changes.

Thank you.
